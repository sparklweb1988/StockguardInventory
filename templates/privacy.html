Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Inventory SaaS, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide while using our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our platform:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and login credentials.
  • Business Data: Product records, stock levels, order details, and invoice data.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you interact with our platform.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the Inventory SaaS platform
  • Manage your account and authenticate users
  • Enable inventory tracking, order management, and invoice generation
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no online platform can guarantee complete security, and users are responsible for protecting their account credentials.

4. Data Sharing

Inventory SaaS does not sell, trade, or rent your personal or business data to third parties.

We may share limited data with trusted service providers only when necessary to operate and maintain the platform (for example hosting or infrastructure services).

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our platform may use cookies or similar technologies to improve functionality, maintain user sessions, and analyze usage patterns.

You can manage cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

7. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we store about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for certain data processing

8. Third-Party Services

Our platform may rely on third-party services such as hosting providers or analytics tools. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
